Sydney Real Estate Analysis

Real-time analysis of property rental market in Sydney, Australia. Weekly rent is modelled using a form of semiparametric regression known as geoadditive models.

U.S. Domestic Flight Data

Real-time analysis of delays in U.S. domestic airline flights using data on flight distance, operating airline, flight path, temperature, wind speed, and aviation flight category.
