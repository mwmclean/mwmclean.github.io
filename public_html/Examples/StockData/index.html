The following two examples analyze stock data market data from both the London Stock Exchange and NASDAQ.

Model Description

The stock price is assumed to follow a normal distribution with variance \(\sigma^2_\epsilon\) and mean \[ E[\log(\text{Microsoft stock price}_{ijk}+120)] = \beta_0+ f_1(\{\text{Intel stock price}\}_j). \]

Real-Time Results

The model is fit (and updated every few minutes) as described in Luts, Broderick, and Wand (2014) .
